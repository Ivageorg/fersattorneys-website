Founder’s Message & Company History

Fers Attorneys was born out of a profound commitment to transform the legal landscape during one of the most challenging periods in recent history—the COVID-19 pandemic. As the global crisis forced widespread office closures and disrupted traditional legal service delivery, it exposed significant gaps in accessibility and efficiency. Witnessing the struggles faced by clients and legal professionals alike, our founder, Ferdinard Nwobodo, a seasoned corporate and litigation lawyer with extensive international credentials, was inspired to create a law firm that could bridge these gaps and redefine the future of legal practice.

Armed with an LLB from Birmingham City University, UK, and certification in Fraud Investigation Management from Coventry University, Ferdinard brought a unique blend of global insight and local expertise to the table. Drawing on his experiences working with renowned law firms and his passion for innovation, he envisioned a modern, technology-driven legal practice designed to transcend traditional constraints to provide seamless, high-quality legal services.

From virtual consultations to streamlined case management, the firm was built to ensure that clients could access expert counsel without disruption, regardless of their location or the complexity of their needs. This founding principle of resilience and innovation continues to drive us today. It shapes our approach to legal practice, guiding our commitment to excellence, flexibility, and client-focused service delivery.

At Fers Attorneys, we are not just responding to the challenges of a changing world—we are redefining how legal services are delivered for generations to come.

Practice Areas

At Fers Attorneys, we pride ourselves on offering an expansive range of legal services. Our multidisciplinary expertise ensures that we provide end-to-end solutions across industries and jurisdictions. Our team of seasoned professionals ensures meticulous attention to detail, proactive strategies, and seamless execution.

1. Corporate & Commercial Law

  • Company Formation & Structuring: Expert guidance on incorporation, regulatory filings, and drafting shareholder agreements.
  • Corporate Governance: Advising boards of directors on compliance, bylaws, and best practices.
  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Comprehensive support, including due diligence, deal structuring, and integration.
  • Commercial Contracts: Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating contracts to safeguard client interests.

2. Property & Real Estate

  • Title Verification: Ensuring clarity of ownership and authenticity of property documentation.
  • Property Transactions: Legal oversight for sales, leases, and financing arrangements.
  • Dispute Resolution: Effective representation in tenancy disputes and enforcement of property rights.
  • Real Estate Development: Advising on permits, financing, and project compliance.

3. Civil Litigation & Debt Recovery

  • Litigation Services: Representation in commercial, contractual, and tort disputes.
  • Debt Recovery: Aggressive yet ethical strategies to recover outstanding debts, including court actions and asset seizure.
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR): Expertise in mediation, arbitration, and negotiation for out-of-court settlements.

4. Immigration Law

  • Work Permits & Expatriate Quotas: Facilitating seamless employment for foreign workers.
  • Visa Services: Assistance with applications, renewals, and residency permits.
  • Naturalisation: Guiding clients through the process of acquiring Nigerian citizenship.
  • Global Mobility: Advising multinational corporations on relocating staff to and from Nigeria.

5. Intellectual Property & Technology

  • IP Registration & Enforcement: Protecting trademarks, patents, and copyrights.
  • Technology Contracts: Drafting IT service agreements, SaaS contracts, and licensing deals.
  • Data Privacy: Ensuring compliance with Nigeria’s NDPR and international data protection standards.

6. Tax & Financial Advisory

  • Tax Planning: Coordinating tax strategies to optimise financial outcomes for businesses and individuals.
  • Corporate Structuring: Designing entities for maximum efficiency and liability protection.
  • Wealth Management: Tailored solutions for high-net-worth individuals.

7. International & Cross-Border Services

  • Foreign Direct Investment (FDI): Assisting international clients in navigating Nigeria’s regulatory framework.
  • Cross-Border Transactions: Drafting and negotiating agreements compliant with Nigerian and foreign laws.
  • International Arbitration: Resolving disputes across jurisdictions with precision and expertise.

8. Estate Planning & Succession

  • Wills & Trusts: Crafting comprehensive estate plans to safeguard family wealth.
  • Family Business Succession: Ensuring smooth transitions for multi-generational enterprises.
  • Asset Protection: Strategies to secure assets against legal and financial risks.

9. Energy & Natural Resources

  • Oil & Gas: Regulatory compliance, licensing, and joint venture agreements.
  • Renewable Energy: Legal advisory for solar, wind, and other sustainable projects.
  • Environmental Compliance: Ensuring adherence to environmental laws.
